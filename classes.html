Beginners

Looking for a new hobby and a chance to meet others with shared interests? Look no futher! Our beginner classes are perfect for those curious to try out pottery for the first time.

Improvers

Looking for an opportunity to build on your skills? Our improvers classes are a wondeful cooperative environment where you will feel confident to take your next steps.

Childrens

Our children's classes nurture your little one's creativity and equip them with the confidence to pursue their dreams. Classes are suitable for ages 3 and up.

Events

Looking for a unique gift, party venue or date night idea? We offer a range of packages to cater for all. Our staff will make sure your special event is one to remember.

Wellbeing Classes

Pottery is hugely beneficial for our wellbeing. Our dedicated classes offer a relaxing and inclusive environment to explore your creativity while taking some time to yourself

Membership

We offer annual memberships where you get up to eight hours of exclusive studio time per week. Ready to level up your skills? Enquire today to find out more!
