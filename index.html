Welcome to South Wales Pottery Club

Set in the heart of the Ogmore Valley, in over an acre of private gardens and woodland, South Wales Pottery Club is a truely unique establishment. We offer classes catering for all levels of experience, so whether you're just starting out or on the hunt for a studio space we can help you discover your passion.

We also host regular events, so if you're looking for a unique day out, the perfect gift for that special someone, something different for date night or even planning your stag or hen do, you'll find plenty of options here. Our staff love nothing more than providing bespoke experiences so you'll be in good hands.

Enquire today to find the perfect class for you!
